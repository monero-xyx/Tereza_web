About Us

Tereza Light Initiative is a nonprofit organization rooted in the heart of Northern Uganda, dedicated to uplifting women, children, and orphans living with HIV/AIDS. We believe that even in the darkest places, light can rise.

Our Programs

  • Healthcare: Providing access to HIV/AIDS treatment and counseling.
  • Education: Supporting vulnerable children’s return to school.
  • Housing: Safe, nurturing environments for women and children.
